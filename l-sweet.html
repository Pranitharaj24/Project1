Love is Sweet

A Chinese Drama(2020)

"Love is Sweet" is a Chinese romantic drama that revolves around the lives of a successful CEO, Yuan Shuai, and a talented pastry chef, Jiang Jun. Despite their differing personalities and career paths, they find themselves drawn to each other. Their journey explores themes of love, ambition, and the challenges of balancing personal and professional lives. As they navigate through obstacles, they learn valuable lessons about trust, forgiveness, and the true meaning of happiness.
